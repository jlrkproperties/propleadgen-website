# Master Replit Prompt — Premium Lead Generation Website

---

## Project Overview

Build a **premium, modern, full-featured marketing website** for a US-focused lead generation and data services company. The business offers three core services:

1. **Real Estate Leads** — Probate, Pre-Probate, Divorce, Eviction, Inheritance, Judgment, Tax Delinquent, Fix & Flip, Distressed Homeowners, Aged Homeowners
2. **Non Real Estate Leads** — Insurance, Mortgage, Refinance, Property Management, Private Lenders, Title Research, Property Analysis, Custom Home Builders
3. **Cold Calling Services** — For Real Estate Agents, Investors & Wholesalers, Cash Buyers, Property Management Companies, Insurance Companies

**Target audience:** US-based real estate investors, wholesalers, agents, insurance companies, mortgage brokers.

**Core positioning:** Quality Over Quantity. Manual verification. No bots, no AI, no recycled lists. Fresh data from official US county/government sources. Delivered in 2–4 days.

## Tech Stack

- **React + Vite** — fast, modern frontend
- **Tailwind CSS** — utility-first styling

## Visual Identity & Design Direction

### Theme
- **Dark, high-contrast, premium** — think intelligence firm, not a freelancer website
- Dark backgrounds (`#0A0A0F`, `#0F1117`) for Hero, Stats, Footer sections
- Light/white (`#FFFFFF`, `#F8F9FA`) for content sections — sandwich structure
- This contrast creates a premium, editorial feel

### Color Palette
```
Primary Dark:     #0A0A0F
Secondary Dark:   #0F1117
Card Background:  #13151C
Border/Subtle:    #1E2130
Accent Primary:   #C8A96E  (gold — authority, premium)
Accent Secondary: #4A9EFF  (electric blue — trust, data)
Text Primary:     #FFFFFF
Text Secondary:   #9CA3AF
Text Muted:       #6B7280
Success Green:    #22C55E
WhatsApp Green:   #25D366
```

### Typography
- **Display/Hero font:** `Playfair Display` (Google Fonts) — authoritative, premium
- **Body font:** `DM Sans` (Google Fonts) — clean, modern, readable
- **Mono/data font:** `JetBrains Mono` — for stats, numbers, data callouts

### Sizing
- Hero title: 64px–80px, bold
- Section titles: 40px–48px
- Card headers: 20px–24px
- Body: 16px–18px
- Captions: 13px–14px

### Motion & Animations
- Smooth scroll-triggered fade-in for all sections (Framer Motion `whileInView`)
- Staggered card reveals — cards animate in one by one, not all at once
- Animated number counters in Stats Bar (count up from 0 on scroll into view)
- Subtle floating/pulse animation on WhatsApp button
- Hover states on all cards — slight lift with gold border glow
- Page transitions — smooth fade between routes

### Visual Motif
- Thin gold (`#C8A96E`) accent lines and borders — consistent across all sections
- Subtle grid/dot pattern as background texture on dark sections
- Rounded corners (`border-radius: 12px`) on all cards
- No decorative full-width colored bars or AI-slop header stripes
